Solitaire Universe has been ported to many devices recently, and we'd like to port to many many more. We need your help though.

If you are willing to help please click here to join our group.

To join the beta group you will need to provide a list of devices that you have for testing. We are looking for phones and tablets running android, webOS, blackberry OS and iOS.

For each device please include the device model name, the OS and OS version that.

You must be willing and able to do the following:

  • Install a package manually
  • Take a screenshot
  • Provide detailed descriptions of bugs

New builds will be posted for each device as they are ready.

Thank you. Your help is greatly appreciated.
