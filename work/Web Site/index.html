A collection of many entertaining types of solitaire, including freecell, klondike, spider, tri-peaks and many more!

Includes many advanced solitaire features:

  • Includes over 60 different types of solitaire including many variants of FreeCell, Klondike, Spider, Canfield, Golf and more!
  • Unlimited undo
  • 5 beautiful cardsets to choose from
  • Many options to make the cards look the way you like them
  • Detailed high scores tracking
  • Player profiles
  • Smart auto play. Tap on a deck of cards to move the whole deck
  • Smart auto drag. The number of cards being drug will automatically adjust based on the destination
  • Play game by number (freecell numbers match other versions of freecell)
